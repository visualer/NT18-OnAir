NT18 OnAir provides the essential information of the NT18 Program on your mobile device, including schedule and poster abstracts, and provides advanced search functionality.

Hope you enjoy your staying here!

Schedule

The detailed schedule of NT18 Program can be found on "schedule" tab.

You can view abstracts of Tutorial, Keynote, Invited talks and Oral presentations by expanding the corresponding event.

Poster Abstracts

Before each poster session, you can search and favorite the poster in which you are interested in advance, and locate them quickly by enabling "favorite-only filter", which can be done by tapping the star button beside the search card.

Wildcard, excluding, and searching within fields are also supported. And by enabling highlight, your search keywords will have more visibility under the yellow background.

Search abstracts

Advanced Searching

If you are trying to find posters by topics, you can filter posters using predefined categories in Topics tab. You can also find posters in a certain session by corresponding entries in the schedule.

And If you are to restrict your search within a field, "author:", "title:", and "content:" keywords should be prefixed, such as "title:MoS2".

Support & Assistance

What if files are not loading, and "Load error" pops up continuously from the bottom? You can just tap the "Report" button on the snackbar and send the error report to accel(at)pku.edu.cn, and the bug will be fixed as soon as possible.

If you still have important technical problems, usage problems or advices which you consider difficult to describe by e-mail, you can contact the developer in person on poster session 2, PC094.
